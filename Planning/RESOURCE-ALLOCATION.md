|------|---------|------------------|--------|

**Potential annual savings:** $17K (3.8% of budget)

### Recommendations

1. **Reserved Instances (RI)** - Commit to 1-year Azure RIs for 30% discount
   - Estimated savings: $10K/year
   - Upfront cost: $5K
   - Payback: 6 months

2. **API Caching Layer** - Reduce API calls by 40% through caching
   - Estimated savings: $3K/year
   - Development effort: 20 hours
   - ROI: Immediate

3. **Open-Source Tools** - Replace paid testing tools with equivalents
   - Estimated savings: $2K/year
   - Setup effort: 40 hours
   - Tradeoff: Less support, self-support required

## Retention & Morale

### Risk Factors

1. **Competitive market** - Mid-market engineers in high demand
2. **Growth pace** - Rapid expansion may create stress
3. **Remote-friendly market** - Losing talent to remote-first companies
4. **Equity options** - Competitors offering equity packages

### Mitigation Strategies

1. **Competitive compensation** - Annual salary benchmarking
2. **Work environment** - Flexible hours, remote options
3. **Growth opportunities** - Clear career progression to senior/lead roles
4. **Equity/Ownership** - Consider stock options for key engineers
5. **Transparent communication** - Regular all-hands, roadmap visibility

**Estimated turnover risk: 10-15% annually** (industry average: 15-20%)
- Plan for 1 replacement hire per year
- Budget: $15K (recruiting + onboarding costs)
