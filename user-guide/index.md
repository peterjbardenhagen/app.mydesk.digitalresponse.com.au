## Getting Started

### 1. Sign In
Navigate to your MyDesk URL and sign in with your corporate email and password. If you forget your password, use the **Forgot password** link.

### 2. Dashboard
After logging in, you land on your **Dashboard**. Here you see:
- Submitted, approved, pending, and reimbursed totals.
- Recent expenses with status badges.
- Quick links to Quotes, Invoices, Contacts, and Reports.

### 3. Expenses
Click **Expenses** in the navigation to view, filter, and search all expense claims. Use the **+ New Expense** button to submit a new claim with a receipt.

### 4. Timesheets
Under **Timesheets**, enter hours against projects and tasks. The weekly grid lets you input daily hours and submit for approval.

### 5. Approvals
The **Approvals** page shows items pending your action. Use the tabs to switch between Expenses & Timesheets, Quotes, Invoices, and Purchase Orders. Approve or reject inline.

### 6. Quotes & Invoices
Create and track quotes and invoices from their respective pages. Monitor statuses from Draft through to Paid or Overdue.

### 7. Notifications
The Notification Bell shows approval requests, budget alerts, and status changes. Mark all as read or click individual notifications for details.

### 8. Profile
Update your personal details, contact information, and password from the **Profile** page.

### Administration

#### Users & Departments
Admins can manage users, departments, and teams from the **Admin** section. Invite users, assign roles, and configure department budgets.

#### Banking
Upload bank statements, review transactions, and match them against invoices and expenses. Reconciliation helps keep your books in sync.

### AI & Automation

#### Ask AI
Use natural language to ask questions about your business data. The AI can summarize sales, list top customers, and suggest actions.

#### AI Agents
Run specialized AI agents for research, quote optimization, and email drafting. Monitor run counts and performance.

#### AgentsOS
Use [AgentsOS](/agentsos) to submit project briefs, monitor AI agent runs, and orchestrate automations across your workspace.
