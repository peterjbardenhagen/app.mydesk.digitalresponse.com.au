**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** July 2026  
**Target Audience:** Executive team, board, investors, product stakeholders

## Executive Summary

MyDesk is positioned as the **enterprise-grade, compliance-first expense management platform** for Australian mid-market organizations. The product captures a fragmented market where companies choose between inflexible enterprise solutions (SAP, Oracle SuccessFactors) or unsecured consumer tools (spreadsheets, email workflows).

**Market Opportunity:** AUD 150M total addressable market (TAM) in Australia alone, with 2000+ companies in the 100-5000 employee range lacking modern, compliant expense management.

**Go-to-Market Strategy:** Land-and-expand via finance/HR buyer, targeting mid-market (500-5000 employees) with pricing starting at AUD 5 per employee per month, bundled with support and compliance reporting.

**Competitive Advantage:** 
1. **Australian-first design** - Data residency, Privacy Act compliance, EOFY reporting
2. **Enterprise security** - ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II, Sarbanes-Oxley ready from day 1
3. **Approval automation** - Smart routing, auto-escalation, delegation
4. **No-code customization** - Clients can modify workflows without developer involvement

## Market Analysis

### Market Size & Opportunity

**Total Addressable Market (TAM): AUD 150M**

Calculation:
- Mid-market companies in Australia (100-5000 employees): 2,400
- Average employees per company: 500
- Total employees across segment: 1.2M
- % with expense management need: ~100% (all organizations)
- Average spend per employee/year: AUD 125 (AUD 10.40/month)
- **TAM = 1.2M × AUD 125 = AUD 150M**

**Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM): AUD 45M**

Assuming we can realistically address:
- Finance-focused organizations with compliance needs: 30% of TAM
- Organizations with established procurement processes: 30% of TAM
- **SAM = TAM × 30% = AUD 45M**

**Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) - 5 Year: AUD 12M**

Conservative acquisition assuming:
- 5 years to establish market presence
- Capture 27% of SAM (100-150 customers at AUD 80k-100k annual contract value)
- **SOM = AUD 12M in 5-year ARR**

### Customer Segments

**Primary: Finance Directors & CFOs (100-5000 employee organizations)**
- Pain: Manual expense processes, audit risk, time-intensive reconciliation
- Budget: Significant (expense management is non-discretionary)
- Decision Cycle: 4-8 weeks (needs financial justification)
- Buyer Power: High (CFO approves budget)

**Secondary: HR Directors (for timesheet management)**
- Pain: Timekeeping scattered across tools, leave tracking manual
- Budget: Moderate (bundled with payroll)
- Decision Cycle: 2-4 weeks
- Buyer Power: Medium (HR proposes, Finance approves)

**Tertiary: Operational Leaders (Managers, Team Leads)**
- Pain: Approval bottlenecks, notification chaos, manual workflows
- Budget: Low (no budget authority)
- Decision Cycle: N/A (not buyers)
- Buyer Power: Low (influencers, not decision-makers)

### Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Positioning | Strengths | Weaknesses | Price |
|------------|-------------|----------|-----------|-------|
| **Expensify** | SMB, consumer-friendly | Mobile-first, receipt OCR | Limited workflow customization, US-centric | $5-10/user/mo |
| **Concur (SAP)** | Enterprise, feature-rich | Comprehensive, strong integration | Expensive, complex, poor UX | $30-50/user/mo |
| **Rydoo** | Mid-market, European | Good mobile, good reporting | Limited approval customization, less secure | AUD 8-12/user/mo |
| **Zoho Expense** | SMB/Mid-market, integrated | Cheap, integrates with Zoho suite | Limited workflows, poor compliance docs | AUD 6-10/user/mo |
| **Local players** | Various | Understand local regs | Limited features, outdated tech, small scale | AUD 15-25/user/mo |
| **Spreadsheets + Email** | DIY | No cost, flexible | Audit risk, zero controls, time-intensive | Free |

**MyDesk Positioning:**
- **Price:** AUD 5/user/mo (aggressive, penetration pricing)
- **Niche:** Australian mid-market with compliance requirements
- **Differentiation:** Security, compliance, approval automation, local support
- **Go-to-Market:** Direct sales to Finance Directors, white-label for HR consultants

### Market Trends Favoring MyDesk

1. **Regulatory Tightening:** Privacy Act amendments, ATO scrutiny on expense controls
2. **Remote Work Growth:** Distributed teams need centralized approval system
3. **Audit Pressure:** ISO 27001, SOC 2 audits increasingly expected
4. **Automation Demand:** Post-COVID focus on operational efficiency
5. **Local Data Requirements:** GDPR alternatives, data sovereignty preferences

## Product Vision & Strategy

### Vision Statement

> "MyDesk is the control center for expense management in Australian enterprises. We make it effortless to submit, approve, and audit expenses—giving CFOs peace of mind, managers clear visibility, and employees fast reimbursement."

### Core Strategic Pillars

**1. Security & Compliance First**
- Every feature designed with compliance in mind
- ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II, Sarbanes-Oxley built-in (not bolted-on)
- Marketing emphasizes "audit-ready from day 1"

**2. Simplicity Through Automation**
- Intelligent approval routing based on amount, category, cost center
- Reduce human decision-making where possible
- One-click approval on mobile
- "Submit in 60 seconds" is the user promise

**3. Enterprise Customization Without Code**
- Configurable workflows, thresholds, approvers
- Template-based notifications
- Custom categories, cost centers per tenant
- "Your policies, our platform" messaging

**4. Australian Market Focus**
- Data residency: Australia-only
- Compliance: Privacy Act, ATO alignment
- Language: English, with colloquial UI ("G'day", "Cheers")
- Support: Australian business hours, local team

### Strategic Objectives (3-Year Roadmap)

**Year 1 (2026) - Establish Product-Market Fit**
- Launch Phase 1-3 (Auth, Expenses, Notifications) ✅ In Progress
- Acquire 10-15 customers (pilot phase)
- Validate product-market fit via NPS > 70
- Achieve ISO 27001 certification
- Revenue: AUD 100k-200k ARR

**Year 2 (2027) - Land & Expand**
- Complete Phase 4-6 (Teams, Dashboard, Enhancements)
- Acquire 50-75 customers total
- Expand features based on customer feedback
- Build app store / partner ecosystem
- Achieve SOC 2 Type II certification
- Revenue: AUD 3-5M ARR

**Year 3 (2028) - Scale & Innovate**
- Complete Phase 7+ (AI, integrations, mobile apps)
- Acquire 150-250 customers total
- Establish market leadership in Australia
- International expansion (NZ, Singapore)
- Revenue: AUD 10-15M ARR

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Sales Motion

**Stage 1: Awareness (Inbound + Events)**
- Content marketing (blog, whitepapers on compliance)
- Thought leadership (conference speaking, industry reports)
- Search marketing (SEO for "expense management compliance")
- Event presence (finance/HR conferences)

**Stage 2: Consideration (Sales Consultation)**
- Free compliance audit (assess current risk)
- ROI calculator (show time/cost savings)
- Live demo with customer's finance team
- Case study from similar industry

**Stage 3: Decision (Proposal + Trial)**
- 30-day free trial with no credit card
- Proposal with compliance roadmap
- Reference call with existing customer
- Implementation timeline and support plan

**Stage 4: Implementation (Onboarding)**
- Dedicated implementation manager
- Training for finance team (2 hours)
- Workflow customization based on their policies
- Data migration from existing system
- Go-live support (48 hours)

### Sales Team Structure

**Year 1:** 1 sales director + 1 sales development rep (SDR)
- Target: 10-15 customers
- Average deal: AUD 50k initial + AUD 10k/year support

**Year 2:** 2 account executives + 2 SDRs + 1 customer success manager
- Target: 50-75 customers
- Reduce new customer acquisition time to 6 weeks

**Year 3:** 5 account executives + 3 SDRs + 2 customer success managers
- Target: 150-250 customers
- Reduce new customer acquisition time to 4 weeks

### Pricing Model

**Primary: Per-Seat SaaS (AUD 5/user/month)**
- Minimum: 50 users (AUD 250/month or AUD 3000/year)
- Volume discounts: 10%+ at 500+ users
- Includes: Core platform, 5GB storage, email support
- Annual contract, billed monthly

**Premium Tier (AUD 10/user/month)** - Coming Year 2
- Everything in Base
- Priority phone support
- Advanced reporting and BI dashboards
- Custom integrations

**Enterprise Tier (Custom Pricing)** - Coming Year 3
- Unlimited users
- On-premise or private cloud option
- Dedicated account team
- SLA guarantees

**Implementation & Support:**
- Standard implementation: AUD 5,000 (included for enterprise)
- Professional services: AUD 200/hour
- Premium support: AUD 5,000/year (included for premium+)

**Pricing Strategy Rationale:**
- AUD 5/user undercuts competitors by 40-50%
- Attracts SMB to mid-market customers
- Higher margins on premium tier
- Land with base tier, expand to premium as customer matures

### Channel Strategy

**Direct Sales (Primary)**
- Sales team targets finance directors directly
- Lead generation via inbound + events
- Largest deals (500+ employees)

**Partner Channel (Secondary) - Year 2+**
- HR Consultancies: White-label, resell with own branding
- Accounting Firms: Recommend to clients, revenue share
- Payroll Providers: Bundle with payroll products
- ERP Resellers: Integrate with QuickBooks, Xero, SAP

## Retention & Expansion Strategy

### Customer Success Playbook

**Onboarding (Week 1-4)**
- Dedicated success manager assigned
- Workflow customization workshop
- Staff training (finance team)
- Go-live support (24/7 for first week)
- Success metric: Team using platform by week 4

**Regular Engagement (Month 2-12)**
- Monthly business reviews (MBR) for large customers
- Quarterly feature updates and training
- Health checks (NPS, usage metrics)
- Proactive support (fixing issues before users report)
- Success metric: NPS > 70

**Expansion (Month 6+)**
- Identify expansion opportunities:
  - Add timesheets (additional AUD 2-5/user/mo)
  - Enable admin users (AUD 100/user/month)
  - Premium reporting (AUD 3-5k/year)
  - Advanced integrations (AUD 5-10k/year)
- Success metric: 30%+ of customers on premium tier by year 2

### Churn Prevention

**Target Churn Rate:** < 5% annual (industry standard: 10-15% for SaaS)

**Churn Risk Indicators:**
- Feature adoption < 40%
- Average daily active users declining
- Support tickets increasing (indicates friction)
- Non-renewal of annual contract

**Churn Mitigation:**
- Proactive outreach to at-risk customers
- Feature requests acted on within 60 days
- Dedicated support for large customers
- Customer advisory board (quarterly feedback)

## Competitive Positioning

### Why MyDesk Over Alternatives

**vs. Expensify**
- ✅ Better approval workflows (Expensify lacks this)
- ✅ Compliance-focused (Expensify skips audit trail)
- ✅ Cheaper for large teams (Expensify per-active-user pricing)
- ❌ Slightly less polished mobile (coming Year 2)

**vs. Concur**
- ✅ 80% cheaper (AUD 5 vs. AUD 30/user)
- ✅ Modern UX (Concur feels enterprise-legacy)
- ✅ Australian data residency (Concur US-only)
- ✅ Faster implementation (4 weeks vs. 16 weeks)
- ❌ Fewer integrations (coming Year 2)

**vs. Zoho Expense**
- ✅ Better approval workflows
- ✅ Compliance reports (audit-ready docs)
- ✅ Australian support (Zoho is India-based)
- ✅ Dedicated product (Zoho = bundled/secondary)
- ❌ Requires separate system (no ERP bundle)

**vs. Spreadsheets**
- ✅ Compliance audit trail (spreadsheets non-compliant)
- ✅ Automated approval routing
- ✅ Receipt OCR (saves 2 hours/week)
- ✅ Mobile approval (any time, anywhere)
- ✅ Scalable (spreadsheets break at 1000+ expenses/year)

### Key Differentiators

1. **Australian-First:** Data residency, Privacy Act, ATO alignment, local support
2. **Compliance-Native:** ISO 27001, SOC 2, Sarbanes-Oxley designed-in
3. **Smart Approval Routing:** Amount-based, cost-center-based, department-based rules
4. **Modern UX:** Built on Blazor/MudBlazor, responsive, fast
5. **Transparent Pricing:** No hidden fees, simple per-seat model

## Risk Analysis & Mitigation

### Key Risks

**Risk 1: Competitive Response**
- **Impact:** High (feature parity, price wars)
- **Likelihood:** Medium (market not crowded yet)
- **Mitigation:** 
  - Build compliance/security moat (hard to copy)
  - Acquire customers quickly (high switching cost)
  - Focus on niche (Australian mid-market)

**Risk 2: Regulatory Change**
- **Impact:** High (requirements could shift)
- **Likelihood:** Low (compliance trends stable)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Maintain compliance buffer (exceed minimums)
  - Build flexible architecture
  - Monitor regulatory landscape quarterly

**Risk 3: Sales Cycle Longer Than Expected**
- **Impact:** Medium (extends timeline to revenue)
- **Likelihood:** Medium (enterprise buyers are slow)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Start with SMB tier (faster sales)
  - Offer free trials (reduce risk)
  - Build strong sales playbook early

**Risk 4: Customer Churn**
- **Impact:** Medium (affects revenue growth)
- **Likelihood:** Medium (SaaS churn typical)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Invest in customer success
  - Regular engagement (MBRs, feature training)
  - Gather feedback, ship requested features

**Risk 5: Technology Disruption**
- **Impact:** Medium (new tech could obsolete platform)
- **Likelihood:** Low (expense management relatively stable)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Build modular architecture (easy to swap components)
  - Invest in AI/ML (future-proof)
  - Monitor emerging tech trends
